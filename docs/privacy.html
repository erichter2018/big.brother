Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Overview

Big Brother ("the App") is a parental control application for iOS and iPadOS. We are committed to protecting the privacy of every family member who uses the App. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is stored, and how it is used.

Data Storage

All data is stored in Apple CloudKit, Apple's first-party cloud infrastructure. Data resides within your family's own iCloud account and is subject to Apple's privacy and security standards. We do not operate any third-party servers, and no family data ever passes through infrastructure outside of Apple's ecosystem.

Data We Collect

The App collects and stores the following data within your family's CloudKit container:

  • Device identifiers — a unique identifier for each enrolled device, used to route commands and track status.
  • Lock mode status — the current enforcement mode for each device (e.g., Unlocked, Locked, Essential Only, Disabled).
  • Heartbeat timestamps — periodic check-in times that indicate a device is online and its configuration is active.
  • Family enrollment data — family identifiers, child profiles, and device-to-child assignments.
  • Parent PIN hash — a PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256 hash stored in the device Keychain. The PIN is never stored in plaintext, and the hash never leaves the device.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect browsing history, messages, photos, or any app content.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect contacts, calendars, or personal files.
  • We do not record screen activity or keystrokes.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or trade your personal data with any third parties. Period. Your data stays within your family's iCloud account and is only accessible to enrolled family members.

Analytics and Tracking

The App contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising frameworks, and no third-party tracking code. We do not use cookies, fingerprinting, or any other tracking mechanism.

Security

  • Parent PINs are hashed using PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256 with 100,000 iterations before storage.
  • PIN hashes are stored exclusively in the iOS Keychain and never transmitted.
  • All CloudKit communication uses Apple's built-in encryption in transit.
  • Policy files stored on-device use atomic writes to prevent corruption.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed to be installed on children's devices by their parents or guardians. The App does not independently collect data from children beyond the device and enrollment information described above. All data collection is initiated and controlled by the parent.

Data Retention and Deletion

Family data persists in CloudKit as long as the family account is active. Parents can unenroll devices and delete family data at any time through the App. When a device is unenrolled, its local policy files and Keychain entries are removed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
