Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Remote Device Management

Control any enrolled device directly from your phone. Change lock modes, check status, and manage settings — all in real time.

Multiple Lock Modes

Four distinct modes — Unlocked, Locked (daily allowed apps), Essential Only, and Disabled — so restrictions match the moment.

Real-Time Monitoring

A heartbeat system keeps you informed of every device's health. Know instantly if a device goes offline or loses its configuration.

Instant Commands

Push-notification-based command delivery means changes take effect in seconds, not minutes. With polling fallback for reliability.

Per-Child & Per-Device

Assign profiles to individual children or specific devices. Each child can have their own rules and allowed apps.

Temporary Unlock

Grant time-limited access that automatically relocks when the window expires. Perfect for homework time or earned screen time.

Privacy First

All data stays in your family's iCloud account via CloudKit. No third-party servers, no analytics SDKs, no data selling — ever.

No Family Sharing Required

Works with any family structure. Enroll devices with simple invite codes — no Apple ID sharing or Family Sharing group needed.

Secure Enrollment

Invite codes, PBKDF2-hashed PINs, and Keychain storage. Security is built into the foundation, not bolted on.
